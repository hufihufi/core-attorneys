## E-Commerce and the digital economy

The digital economy presents companies with new competition law and regulatory challenges. CORE Attorneys supports companies in this dynamic environment.

- Platform regulation and digital markets (DMA)
- Competition law for digital business models
- Pricing algorithms and algorithmic collusion
- Online distribution systems and marketplace restrictions
- Data access and data portability
